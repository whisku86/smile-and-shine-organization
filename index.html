Our Story

Who We Are &
What We Stand For

The Smile and Shine Girls Foundation is dedicated to creating a world where every girl has the opportunity to shine. We focus on empowering marginalised communities through education, mentorship, and sustainable development initiatives across Kenya.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to empowering marginalized communities in Kenya through sustainable development, advocacy, and driving lasting positive change for girls and their families.

Our Vision

A world where every girl has the opportunity to shine, with access to quality education, mentorship, and sustainable development — where every girl can lead and transform her community.

"At Smile and Shine Girls Foundation, we believe in the power of education and mentorship to transform lives. We are committed to creating a future where every girl can thrive, lead, and contribute meaningfully to her community."
Founder, Smile and Shine Girls Foundation
